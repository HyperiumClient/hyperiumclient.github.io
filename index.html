Hypixel Community Client

Free client with HUDs and modules with gameplay optimization

Features

  • Popular community's mod integrations: Levelhead, AutoTip, AutoGG, Chromahud 3.0 and More!
  • Integrated MemoryFix and MouseFixDelay, thanks to prplz
  • Discord rich presence, Show details of server you're on
  • Configurable settings with nice looking GUI Menu
  • Addons, You could make your own addon to the client!
  • The client can be used as Standalone or Forge mod
  • The client is completely free! Its written in Java and Kotlin
